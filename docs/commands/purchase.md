# !purchase

Purchase raffle tickets during special giveaways.

Converts `currency` into `tickets`.

---

#### Usage
`!purchase`__*`<amount> <ticket_type>`*__

|Parameter|Type|
|---|---|
|__amount__|_number_|
|__ticket_type__|_`ffxiv`_, _`gw2`_|

__Examples__:

`!purchase 2 gw2`

`!purchase 3 ffxiv`
